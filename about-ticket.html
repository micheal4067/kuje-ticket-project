About Ticket

Ticket is a web-based application designed to streamline ticket issuance for vehicles at a park. The app allows users to customize their enterprise name and set fees for various vehicle types. With a single click on a vehicle button, the system generates a receipt ready for printing, displaying details such as the fee, date, and time.

Key Features Include:

  • User Login and Tracking: Upon login, the system records and displays the user's login time and date on the dashboard.
  • Sales Management: Users can view daily sales records and print detailed receipts that include the total sales amount, login details, and receipt generation time.
  • Data Reset on Logout: Logging out clears the day's sales data and resets records (like the last login time) to prepare for a new day's transactions.
  • Sales History: Users can access a comprehensive view of all past sales, with options to save reports as PDF files or delete records after auditing.

Ticket simplifies park operations with intuitive features, ensuring efficient and organized ticket and sales management.
